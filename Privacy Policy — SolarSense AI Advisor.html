Privacy Policy

SolarSense AI Advisor · Effective 16 May 2026

SolarSense AI Advisor (the "Service") is a demonstration project built for a graduate-level coursework assignment at the National University of Singapore, illustrating an AI-powered Power Purchase Agreement origination tool for Sembcorp Industries' commercial and industrial solar business.

What this Service does

The Service takes a site address and a few basic details from the user and returns a non-binding indicative solar PPA quote, drawing on Google's Geocoding API and Google's Solar API for rooftop analysis. Conversational responses are generated by OpenAI's ChatGPT platform under a custom configuration.

What we collect

The Service only processes information the user explicitly types into the chat (e.g. address, monthly bill, industry, rooftop area). Address strings are passed to Google's Maps Platform APIs for geocoding and rooftop solar analysis. Conversational text is processed by ChatGPT.

No personally identifying information beyond what the user volunteers is collected. The Service does not request, store, or process payment information, identity documents, contact lists, location data from the device, or any other sensitive personal information.

What we do not do

The Service does not persist user inputs in any database controlled by us. We do not sell, share, or transfer user data to advertising networks. We do not use cookies or analytics beyond what is intrinsic to the platforms (OpenAI ChatGPT, Google Maps Platform) that the Service is built on.

Third-party services

The Service depends on the following third parties, each of whom has their own privacy policy that governs their handling of the data they receive:

  • OpenAI (ChatGPT, the Custom GPT platform) — openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
  • Google Maps Platform (Geocoding API, Solar API) — policies.google.com/privacy

Retention

The Service itself retains no data. Conversational history is held by OpenAI under its own retention policies and may be reviewed by the user from within their ChatGPT account.
